3-Tab vs Architectural Shingles

Modern architectural (dimensional) shingles have largely replaced traditional 3-tab shingles in Foster City and throughout the Bay Area. Here is why:

  • 3-tab shingles — Flat, uniform appearance. 20 to 25-year warranty. Lower cost. Still available for budget projects and rental properties. Limited color options.
  • Architectural shingles — Multi-layered construction creates a dimensional, textured look that mimics wood shake or slate. 30 to 50-year warranties. Superior wind resistance (110 to 130 MPH). More color choices. Only slightly more expensive than 3-tab — the price difference has narrowed significantly. We recommend architectural shingles for nearly every Foster City home.
