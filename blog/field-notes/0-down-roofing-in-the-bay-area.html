Pay Nothing Up Front Until City Inspection Passes

When it comes to replacing your roof, many homeowners worry about one thing more than anything else: paying thousands of dollars before knowing the job is done right. At ROOF EXPRESS, we eliminate that concern with our customer-friendly financing policy: $0 down payment until your city inspection has passed.

What Does “Pay After City Inspection” Mean?

Most roofing replacement projects in cities such as San Francisco, Oakland, San Jose, and throughout the Bay Area require a permit and final inspection by the local building department.

After we complete your roof replacement:

We schedule the final inspection.

A city inspector reviews the installation.

The inspector confirms the roof meets all building code requirements.

You receive the signed-off permit.

You make payment only after approval.

This process ensures an independent third party verifies that your roof was installed correctly.
