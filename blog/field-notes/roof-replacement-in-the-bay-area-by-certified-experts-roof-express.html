When it comes to protecting your property, your roof is the first line of defense. Whether your roof is aging, leaking, or has suffered damage, our team ensures a smooth and reliable replacement process from start to finish.

A full roof replacement is one of the most important investments you can make in your home or commercial building. It helps prevent water damage, improves energy efficiency, increases property value, and enhances curb appeal. Many homeowners wait until problems become severe, but replacing your roof at the right time can save thousands in future repairs.

ROOF EXPRESS is proudly certified by the top three roofing manufacturers in the industry. We are a GAF Certified Installer, Owens Corning Preferred Contractor, and CertainTeed Certified Contractor. These certifications are not easy to achieve—they require strict training, proven experience, and consistent high-quality workmanship.

As a result, our customers benefit from superior installation standards and access to enhanced manufacturer warranties.

Our roof replacement process is designed to be efficient, clean, and stress-free. We begin with a detailed inspection of your existing roof to assess its condition. Once replacement is confirmed, we carefully remove all old roofing materials and inspect the decking underneath.

If needed, we replace damaged plywood to ensure a solid foundation. We then install a complete roofing system using premium materials from trusted brands like GAF, Owens Corning, and CertainTeed. After installation, we conduct a final quality inspection and register your warranty for long-term protection.

We specialize in a wide range of roofing systems, including asphalt shingle roofs, flat roofing systems such as torch-down and TPO, residential homes, multi-family properties, and commercial buildings. No matter the size or complexity of the project, ROOF EXPRESS delivers consistent, high-quality results.

Choosing the right roofing contractor is critical. ROOF EXPRESS is a Diamond Certified roofing company known for professionalism, transparency, and reliability. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured, and we understand the unique climate conditions of the Bay Area—from coastal moisture in San Francisco to heat variations in inland cities.

Our experience allows us to recommend the best roofing solutions for long-term performance.

There are several signs that indicate it may be time for a roof replacement. These include missing or curling shingles, frequent leaks, water stains on ceilings, a roof that is over 20–25 years old, sagging areas, or rising energy bills. If you notice any of these issues, it is important to schedule a professional inspection as soon as possible.
