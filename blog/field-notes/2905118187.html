Chimney Leak Repair in Daly City (2026 Guide) Why Chimney Leaks Happen & Why Fast Repair Is Critical

Chimney leaks are one of the most common roofing problems in Daly City. With strong coastal winds, frequent fog, and heavy winter rain, chimneys are highly exposed to moisture. If flashing or masonry fails, water can quickly enter your attic, ceilings, and walls.

Timely chimney leak repair protects your roof structure, prevents mold growth, and avoids costly interior damage.

Common Causes of Chimney Leaks 1️⃣ Damaged or Improper Flashing

Flashing seals the gap between the chimney and roof. If it lifts, cracks, or rusts, water enters immediately.

2️⃣ Cracked Mortar or Masonry

Over time, brick chimneys absorb moisture. Cracked mortar joints allow water to penetrate.

3️⃣ Worn Chimney Cap

A damaged or missing chimney cap allows rainwater to flow directly into the flue.

4️⃣ Missing Ice & Water Barrier

Improper underlayment around the chimney base increases leak risk.

5️⃣ Sealant Failure

Old caulking dries and cracks, breaking the waterproof seal.

Signs Your Chimney Is Leaking

Water stains near fireplace walls

Ceiling stains near chimney area

Musty attic smell

Dripping during rain

Rusted flashing

Peeling paint near chimney

Leaks may appear far from the actual chimney location due to water traveling along framing.

Professional Chimney Leak Repair Process 1️⃣ Roof & Flashing Inspection

We inspect step flashing, counter flashing, and surrounding shingles.

2️⃣ Flashing Repair or Replacement

Damaged flashing is removed and replaced with properly layered metal flashing.

3️⃣ Waterproof Underlayment Upgrade

Ice & water barrier may be installed around the chimney base for extra protection.

4️⃣ Masonry Sealing (If Needed)

Brick or mortar cracks may require sealing or repair.

5️⃣ Final Waterproof Testing

We ensure all transitions are sealed and secure.

Extremely important. Chimneys are one of the most vulnerable roof penetration points. Improper DIY repairs often fail because they rely on surface sealants instead of proper flashing replacement.

Chimney Leak Repair FAQ

Can chimney leaks be fixed without replacing the roof?

Yes, if the surrounding shingles and decking are in good condition.

Is flashing replacement expensive?

Cost depends on damage extent, but early repair is much cheaper than structural repairs.

How long does chimney repair take?

Most repairs are completed within a day.
