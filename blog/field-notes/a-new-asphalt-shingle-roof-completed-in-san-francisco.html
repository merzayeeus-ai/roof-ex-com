Why Asphalt Shingle Roofs Are Popular in San Francisco

Architectural asphalt shingles remain one of the most trusted roofing systems for Bay Area homes because they offer:

✔ Strong wind resistance

✔ Excellent waterproof protection

✔ Cost-effective installation

✔ Long lifespan (20-30+ years)

✔ Beautiful appearance that matches San Francisco architecture

For homes located on hillsides or exposed to strong coastal winds, a properly installed shingle system is essential for preventing leaks and structural damage.

Importance of Proper Vent Pipe Flashing

One of the most common causes of roof leaks is improper waterproofing around roof penetrations such as vent pipes.

During this project, the ROOF EXPRESS team ensured that:

Pipe boots were properly sealed

Flashing was integrated with the shingle system

Waterproof layers were installed beneath the shingles

This ensures long-term protection and prevents moisture intrusion.
