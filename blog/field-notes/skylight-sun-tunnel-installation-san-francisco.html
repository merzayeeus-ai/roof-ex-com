Skylights vs. Sun Tunnels – What’s the Difference? Choosing between a skylight and a sun tunnel depends on your space, budget, and lighting needs. Skylights

Ideal for living rooms, kitchens, and large spaces

Provides direct natural light and sky views

Can be fixed or vented for airflow

Adds value and architectural appeal

Sun Tunnels (Solar Tubes)

Perfect for hallways, bathrooms, and small spaces

Uses a reflective tube to channel sunlight

More affordable and easier to install

Great solution where traditional skylights won’t fit
