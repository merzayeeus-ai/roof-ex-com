Flat Roofing for Coastal Conditions

Pescadero’s coastal air, fog, and seasonal storms can be tough on a roof. That’s why a proper flat roof installation focuses on:

Waterproofing and seam integrity

Correct slope and drainage planning

High-quality underlayment and flashing

Durable membranes designed for low-slope roofs

Flat Roof Installation Options

We install and replace a variety of flat roofing systems, depending on your building type and budget, including:

TPO Roofing – energy-efficient, heat-reflective, and great for long-term performance

Modified Bitumen – strong, reliable, and ideal for many residential flat roofs

Torch-Down Roofing – a proven system for waterproof protection on low-slope structures

Silicone Roof Coatings – an option for added protection and improved longevity (when appropriate)

What’s Included in a New Flat Roof Installation

Our flat roof installation process is designed for lasting results:

Inspection and project planning

Removal of old materials (if needed)

Deck preparation and repair

Moisture barrier and insulation (as required)

Membrane installation with sealed seams

New flashing around edges, vents, skylights, and transitions

Drainage improvements (scuppers, gutters, downspouts, or internal drains)

Final walkthrough and cleanup

Why Invest in a New Flat Roof?

A professionally installed flat roof can deliver:

Reliable leak protection

Lower maintenance over time

Clean, modern curb appeal

Better energy efficiency with reflective systems

A strong base for solar or rooftop equipment
