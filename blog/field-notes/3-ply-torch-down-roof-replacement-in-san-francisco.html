3-Ply Torch Down Roof Replacement in San Francisco

Complete Flat Roof Upgrade by ROOF EXPRESS

Flat roofs in San Francisco face constant exposure to moisture, fog, UV rays, and temperature fluctuations. Over time, these conditions break down older roofing systems, leading to leaks, ponding water, and structural damage. At ROOF EXPRESS, we recently completed a full 3-ply torch-down roof replacement project, transforming a failing flat roof into a durable, waterproof system built to last.

This project highlights the importance of proper installation, high-quality materials, and experienced craftsmanship when dealing with flat roofing systems in the Bay Area.

Before: Failing Torch Down Roof System

The original roof had reached the end of its lifespan. Multiple layers of aged modified bitumen were deteriorating, with visible cracks, blisters, and soft spots throughout the surface. Water infiltration had already begun, and the decking underneath showed signs of damage.

Our inspection identified several key issues:

Advanced wear and tear from UV exposure

Poor drainage causing ponding water

Compromised seams and flashing points

Rotten and weakened plywood decking

Improper previous repairs

At this stage, a full roof replacement was the only reliable solution.

Scope of Work – Complete Flat Roof Replacement

ROOF EXPRESS performed a full tear-off and installation of a new 3-ply torch-down roofing system, following industry best practices and San Francisco building requirements.

The project included:

Full removal of existing roofing materials

Replacement of damaged plywood decking with 5/8” CDX exterior-grade sheets

Installation of new drip edge and metal flashings

Installation of proper ventilation components

Application of a high-performance 3-layer torch-down system

Reinforcement of all seams and penetrations

Waterproof sealing around vents, pipes, and edges

Every step was completed with precision to ensure long-term performance and compliance.

What Is a 3-Ply Torch Down Roofing System?

A 3-ply torch-down system is one of the most reliable flat roofing solutions for homes and buildings in San Francisco. It consists of three layers of modified bitumen applied using heat (torch method), creating a seamless, waterproof membrane.

Benefits of a 3-ply system:

Superior waterproof protection

High resistance to UV and weather damage

Long lifespan (20+ years with proper maintenance)

Excellent performance in foggy and rainy climates

Strong protection against leaks and ponding water

This system is especially ideal for San Francisco’s climate, where moisture control is critical.
