Roofing Designed for San Francisco Weather

San Francisco roofs experience heavy moisture exposure from fog, rain, salt air, and changing temperatures. Without proper installation and drainage, roofs can develop leaks, ponding water, mold, and structural damage over time.

ROOF EXPRESS focuses on proper waterproofing, high-quality underlayment systems, flashing protection, and drainage design to help roofs perform efficiently throughout the year. Our team carefully evaluates every roof structure to identify potential weak points before they become expensive problems.
