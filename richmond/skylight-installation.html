Skylight Types We Install

We install the complete line of VELUX skylights in Richmond homes and commercial buildings:

  • Fixed skylights — Bring light into rooms that do not need ventilation. Perfect for hallways, closets, and stairwells. Available in multiple sizes.
  • Manual venting skylights — Open with a hand crank for natural airflow. Ideal for kitchens and bathrooms where moisture needs to escape.
